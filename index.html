Primary and Secondary

A school is an educational institution where students of various ages learn and develop knowledge, skills and social abilities under the guidance of teachers. Schools typically offer structured curriculums covering subjects like mathematics, science and arts are divided into levels such as primary, secondary.

Higher Secondary

Higher secondary education refers to the stage of formal education that follows secondary school and typically includes grades 11 and 12. It serves as a bridge between secondary education and higher studies, providing students with in-depth knowledge in specialized streams such as science, commerce, or humanities.

Bachelor's Degree

A Bachelor's degree is an undergraduate academic qualification awarded by universities or colleges upon completing a specified program of study, typically lasting 3 to 4 years. It provides foundational knowledge and skills in a particular field such as science, arts, business or engineering.
